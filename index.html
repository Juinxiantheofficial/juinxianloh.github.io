About Me

Background

I'm Juin Xian Loh, a research scientist specialising in sustainable aviation fuel (SAF) — one of the most critical levers for decarbonising commercial aviation. My work sits at the intersection of chemistry, engineering, and energy systems.

Alongside my research, I serve as an associate in technology policy and governance at the Asia SAF Association, translating scientific findings into actionable frameworks for Asia Pacific's aviation sector.

Bridging rigorous science and thoughtful governance is what drives me — and outside the lab, sport and music keep me grounded.
